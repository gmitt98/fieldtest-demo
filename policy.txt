ACME STORE — CUSTOMER SERVICE POLICY
=====================================

RETURNS & REFUNDS
-----------------
- Items may be returned within 30 days of purchase with receipt.
- Items must be unused and in original packaging.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
- Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned.
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

SHIPPING
--------
- Standard shipping: 5–7 business days.
- Expedited shipping: 2–3 business days (additional fee applies).
- Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping.
- We ship to the contiguous US only. No international shipping.
- Once an order ships, we cannot change the delivery address.

DAMAGED OR DEFECTIVE ITEMS
---------------------------
- Contact us within 7 days of delivery if an item arrives damaged or defective.
- We will replace the item or issue a full refund, including shipping.
- Do not return damaged items without contacting us first.

CANCELLATIONS
-------------
- Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement.
- After 1 hour, orders enter fulfillment and cannot be cancelled.

CONTACT
-------
- Customer service is available Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm EST.
- Response time is typically 1 business day.
